About Me

I am a fifth-year Ph.D. candidate in Applied and Computational Mathematics at the University of Wisconsin-Madison.

My research interests include scientific machine learning and inverse problems.

I am advised by Dr. Qin Li and Dr. Leonardo Andrés Zepeda Núñez.
